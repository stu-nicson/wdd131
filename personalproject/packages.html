Package Details

Which package is right for you?

The right choice depends on your home size, priorities, and budget. These package options help simplify that decision.

Starter Package

A clean first step into smart home living

The Starter package is built for people who want to begin with the most approachable side of smart home technology. It works well for apartments, smaller homes, or anyone who wants better convenience through lighting, smart plugs, and simple assistant integration.

This package keeps things easy to manage while still providing a visible upgrade in comfort and control.

Security Package

Focused on awareness, access, and protection

The Security package is the best fit for customers who care most about seeing what is happening around the home and controlling who has access. Smart locks, doorbell cameras, and sensors create a stronger sense of day-to-day awareness.

This option is especially useful for families, homeowners, and customers who want a more practical and reassuring setup.

Premium Package

A more complete connected home system

The Premium package is designed for customers who want their smart home devices to work together in a more complete and personalized way. It combines lighting, security, and automation features into one cohesive system.

This package is best for larger homes or customers who want a stronger long-term smart home setup instead of just one or two isolated upgrades.
