Lighting

Smart Lighting Installation

Smart lighting is one of the easiest ways to upgrade a home. Customers can turn lights on and off remotely, set schedules for different times of day, and create scenes for relaxing, working, or entertaining. These systems can also support better energy habits by reducing wasted lighting throughout the day.

This service is a great fit for people who want a clean first step into smart home technology without committing to a full whole-home system right away.

Security

Smart Locks and Entry Security

Smart security tools help homeowners keep track of entrances, improve awareness, and simplify access management. Smart locks can remove the need for physical keys, while entry sensors can notify homeowners when doors or windows are opened.

This service works well for families, renters, and homeowners who want better day-to-day peace of mind and more control over who can access their home.

Monitoring

Cameras and Video Doorbells

Cameras and video doorbells provide real-time visibility into what is happening around the home. They are especially useful for front doors, driveways, garages, and other key entry points. A customer can check footage, receive notifications, and review activity from their phone.

This service is ideal for people who want stronger visibility, package monitoring, or a more complete home security setup.

Automation

Connected Home Automation

Home automation brings multiple smart devices together so they can respond automatically to routines and conditions. For example, lights can turn on at sunset, entry lighting can react to motion, and devices can be grouped into morning, away, or nighttime modes.

This service is best for customers who want their home to feel smoother, smarter, and more personalized rather than just adding isolated smart gadgets.

Integration

Voice Assistant Setup

Voice assistant integration helps bring smart devices into one easy-to-use system. Customers can use voice commands to adjust lighting, check device status, or activate routines without needing to open multiple apps.

This service is especially useful for households that want more convenience, accessibility, and easier control over connected devices.
